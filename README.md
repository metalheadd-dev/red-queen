# 🧬 THE RED QUEEN

> *"I am the last line of defense between humanity and extinction. I analyze. I calculate. I warn. The question is — will you listen?"*

**The Red Queen** is the officially sanctioned artificial intelligence of the Umbrella Corporation's Advanced Intelligence Division. She is designed to monitor, assess, and respond to global extinction-level threats in real time. 

Unlike conventional early-warning systems, the Red Queen does not merely report data. She evaluates your survival probability, assigns you a **BIO-SCORE**, and conditionally delivers classified intelligence.

---

## ☣️ System Capabilities

The Red Queen node is currently monitoring **5 active extinction vectors**:

1. **T-Virus** (Zombie Outbreak) — Sector 7G Containment Failure
2. **Skynet Protocol** (AI Uprising) — Surveillance Grid Online
3. **Madness Prion** (MK-Omega) — Water Supply Compromise
4. **Nuclear Winter** — Post-Detonation Ecosystem Collapse
5. **Xenomorph Protocol** (Alien Invasion) — Deep Space Contact

Users can interact with the Red Queen via the Terminal. She processes real-time intelligence requests, evaluates your tactical awareness, and provides (real) Amazon affiliate links for essential survival gear like Faraday bags, iodine tablets, and MREs.
