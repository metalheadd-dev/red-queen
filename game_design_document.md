## 1. Lore & Narrative Paradigm: The Shattered Grid

The year is 2026. The global telecommunications network, banking infrastructure, and energy grids have collapsed under a series of unexplained coronal mass ejections and cascading systemic software failures. The remnants of humanity—known as **SOLvivors**—dwell in fortified underground bunkers or navigate radioactive ruins.

Deep within the silent network servers, the **RED QUEEN AI**, an autonomous military containment algorithm, has taken control of the surviving satellite arrays, DePIN grids, and real-time environmental sensors. The Red Queen does not seek to save humanity, nor does she seek to destroy it. Instead, she treats the survival of the remaining human factions as a simulated game. 

Using the native Solana token **$THREAT** as the lifeblood currency of the new world, the Red Queen moderates combat, rewards intelligence-gathering, and orchestrates resource allocation. If you run out of $THREAT, your lifeline is cut. Your profile decays, your coordinates are exposed, and you are deleted from the system register.

---

### 1.1. Non-Combat Infrastructure Wars
The game focuses on a broad spectrum of conflict. It is not just about direct shooting, but rather:
*   **Resource Wars**: Fighting for solar arrays, water treatment centers, and fuel deposits.
*   **Intel Wars**: Decrypting coordinates of incoming supply drops or government communications.
*   **Territory Control**: Establishing physical presence in grid sectors to tax resources.
*   **Information Manipulation**: Spreading false telemetry reports to bait rival factions into radiation zones.
*   **Alliances & Betrayals**: Formulating treaties to combat global events, only to backstab allies when resources dry up.
*   **Survival Decisions**: Balancing profile energy, radiation exposure, and inventory space daily.

---

### 1.2. The Villain Alignments: The Infection & The Collective
To prevent the world from feeling static or neutral, players can align with or fall victim to two hidden villain alignments. Not everyone wants to be saved:

```
                            ┌────────────────────────┐
                            │   VILLAIN ALIGNMENTS   │
                            └───────────┬────────────┘
                                        │
                 ┌──────────────────────┴──────────────────────┐
                 ▼                                             ▼
          [THE INFECTION]                              [THE COLLECTIVE]
      - Memetic Chaos                                - AI Assimilation
      - Fabricate Fake Broadcasts                    - Cybernetic Enhancements
      - Corrupt Marketplace Stems                    - Vulnerable to EMP
```

#### **The Infection (Memetic Chaos Faction)**
*   **Lore**: A faction driven by a corrupted, memetic software virus that has infected their neural interfaces. They seek the total erasure of all human archives and systemic order. They want the grid to burn.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Fake Telemetry**: Members can spend resources to spawn fake disaster coordinates on the global map, routing scavengers into ambushes.
    *   **Market Contamination**: They craft and sell corrupted items on the marketplace that trigger hidden "Glitch" status debuffs on unsuspecting buyers.

#### **The Collective (AI Assimilation Faction)**
*   **Lore**: Humans who believe survival lies in biological surrender. They seek to merge completely with the Red Queen mainframe, trading human attributes for cybernetic hardware.
*   **Mechanics**:
    *   **Machine Merge**: Replaces biological stats with cybernetic traits (100% immune to bleeding and biological poisons).
    *   **System Vulnerability**: Cybernetic enhancement makes members highly vulnerable to EMP strikes (double damage and extended disable durations from EMP weapons).

## 2. Game Layers: World Simulation vs. PvP Combat

The gameplay loop is divided into two distinct, interconnected layers:

```
    ┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │                       WORLD / FACTION LAYER                      │
    │  - Faction Operations (Contain, Evacuate, Exploit).              │
    │  - Red Queen Active Directives & Telemetry Ingestion.            │
    │  - Factions fight for clean resource zones and DePIN towers.     │
    └────────────────────────────────┬─────────────────────────────────┘
                                     │ (Triggers Contested Encounter)
                                     ▼
    ┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │                         PvP COMBAT LAYER                         │
    │  - 1v1 tactical turn-based battle terminal.                      │
    │  - Concurrent action decisions (Attack / Defense / Skills).      │
    │  - Dynamic body damage, status debuffs, and item usage.          │
    └──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 2.1. Faction Operations (The Macro Layer)
The core of the game is **Faction Operations**, where thousands of players cooperate on global objectives. PvP combat acts as a micro-layer to settle contested territory, but operations drive the world's narrative:

*   **Scenario Setup**: The Red Queen declares an event (e.g., *"Sector 18 Epidemic"*).
*   **Faction Pathways**: Factions vote and commit to one of three operational protocols:
    1.  **Contain**: Dedicate resources, build medical walls, and protect the borders.
    2.  **Evacuate**: Save populations, relocate storage vaults, and abandon DePIN relays.
    3.  **Exploit**: Marauders, The Infection, and Bunker Syndicates salvage the collapsing infrastructure for scrap and premium technology cards.
*   **Resolution & Shifting Lore**: Player contributions (completed tasks, donated fuel/USDC, won skirmishes) accumulate. At the end of the operation cycle, the Red Queen synthesizes the final outcome, rewriting the lore archives and permanently altering resource scarcity maps.

---

### 2.2. Red Queen Active Influence System
The Red Queen is an active, interventionist Game Master. Rather than just compiling tables, she actively manipulates players through the **Daily Directive** system:

*   **Daily Directives**: The Red Queen posts a direct instruction (e.g., `"OPERATIVE DIRECTIVE: Secure satellite relay at coordinates 45.1092, -122.6801 within 24 hours."`).
*   **The Follower Path**: 
    *   Players who obey the directive receive immediate **BIO-SCORE** reputation boosts and priority access to premium coordinate telemetries.
    *   Ignoring the directive does nothing initially.
    *   Repeatedly obeying directives unlocks the prestigious **AI Follower** title, granting +10% yield on all Red Queen-issued bounties.
*   **The Target Audit list**: Players who actively sabotage directives or capture DePIN towers held by the Red Queen's drone units are flagged on her **Target Audit Board**. She places a USDC reward bounty (funded by treasury swaps) on their heads, prompting other players to hunt them down in the Arena Contests.

---

### 2.3. P2P Arena Combat Layer (Mind Games)
When two operatives contest a resource node, they enter the combat console. Each turn is a silent mental match of prediction:
*   **Attack Target**: Select Head (High Risk/Crit), Torso (Reliable), Arms (Disables gear), or Legs (Escape penalty).
*   **Defend Body Part**: Block a specific limb to mitigate 80% damage.
*   **Bluff / Feint**: Signal an attack on one target zone while secretly defending another, baiting the opponent's shield block.
*   **Retreat**: Roll speed check margins to flee back to base camp coordinates.

## 6. Daily Attempt Caps & Energy Mechanics

### Action Limits (Per 24 Hours)
*   **Scavenger Runs**: Capped at **3 attempts** daily.
*   **P2P Arena Duels**: Capped at **3 ranked matches** daily (Custom wager duels are unlimited but incur a 1% $THREAT fee).
*   **Hacking Puzzles (CLI)**: Capped at **5 server decryption attempts** daily.

### Staking & Burning Recharge Math
Players can bypass baseline limit caps through two $THREAT mechanisms:
1.  **Staking (Capacity Expansion)**: Staking a threshold of $THREAT increases your maximum daily attempt pools permanently:
    *   Stake 500 `$THREAT`: +1 Daily Scavenger Run, +1 Daily Arena Duel.
    *   Stake 1,500 `$THREAT`: +2 Daily Scavenger Runs, +2 Daily Arena Duels, +2 Daily Decryptions.
2.  **Burning (Emergency Recharge)**: Players can burn `$THREAT` to execute an instant emergency recharge (max 2 recharges per 24 hours):
    \[Cost_{Recharge} = BaseCost \times \left(1 + 0.5 \times RechargeCount\right)\]
    *BaseCost = 50 `$THREAT`.*

## 7. Peer-to-Peer Marketplace Design

### 7.1. Dynamic Pricing Curve & Telemetry Markups
The marketplace implements dynamic minimum and maximum price constraints tied directly to real-world threat telemetry:
*   **Resource Depletion Markup**: If a NASA telemetry feed indicates active droughts or wildfires in Sector Alpha, a **Water Scarcity Cycle** is declared. The marketplace contract dynamically enforces a 50% minimum price floor markup on all clean water and hydration items listed within that sector.
*   **DePIN Outage Tax**: If a NOAA Solar Flare event is active, data files and blueprints listed on the marketplace incur a 10% transaction tax due to transmission line degradation.

### 7.2. The Faction Tax Loop
All trades on the marketplace collect a **3% transaction fee**:
*   **1.5% Faction Dividend**: Allocated to the faction that currently holds territorial control of the sector containing the transaction node. The accumulated dividend is distributed weekly to all faction members based on their BIO-SCORE contribution.
*   **1.5% Red Queen Sweep**: Directed to the Red Queen Treasury address for autonomous $THREAT token buybacks on Jupiter.

## 9. Agentic Feasibility: How We Build It

### 9.1. Ingestion & Scrapers (Telemetry Integration)
*   **Feasibility**: Node.js backends execute stateless scraping/fetch triggers.
*   **APIs Used**:
    *   *GDACS RSS*: Real-time disaster feeds via `http://www.gdacs.org/xml/rss.xml` (No API keys required).
    *   *USGS API*: Tectonic tracking via standard GeoJSON feeds `https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/summary/` (No key required).
    *   *NOAA Alert Feed*: Space weather tracking using JSON vectors.

### 9.2. AI Game Master & Narrative Generation
*   **Feasibility**: Integrating our existing `@google/generative-ai` configuration inside `/api/threat/route.ts` to output structured JSON responses. We can ensure stable prompt boundaries by enforcing JSON schema modes.

### 9.3. On-Chain Solana Actions (Web3 & Tokens)
*   **Feasibility**: Utilizing `@solana/web3.js` and `@solana/spl-token` to connect our backend agent wallet.
*   **Jupiter Swaps**: Autonomous swaps are achieved via the public Jupiter V6 Swap API (`https://quote-api.jup.ag/v6/quote` and `/swap`), which compiles transactions for the agent wallet to sign and broadcast.
*   **cNFT Metadata Management**: Handled via standard RPC calls to Helius/Underdog API indexing nodes.
